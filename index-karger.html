Expressions

  • Spreadsheet-style expression syntax
  • But instead of grid cells, refer to properties by name
  • If referred property is multiple, aggregate functions like sum

Evaluation

20 Participants

  • recruited via web designer media/meetup groups
  • 7 male, 12 female, 1 other
  • self described intermediate or better with HTML
  • 13 beginner or worse programmers, 7 intermediate
  • Asked to bring HTML mockup of contact manager app

Study Design

  • Tutorial (45 min)
  • Structured series of Tasks (~20 minutes)
    • two distinct apps
    • Increasing difficulty
    • First, basic content management
    • Followed by basic expressions
    • Followed by aggregates and conditionals
  • Unstructured study of their contact manager

Results

  • 17 subjects did a structured task
  • 100% success on data editing tasks (in 20 minutes)
  • 97% success on basic expressions
  • 75% success on complex expressions
  • 2 point drop in perceived difficulty on 5-point Likert scale


Freestyle study

Results

  • 7 subjects did a freestyle task
  • All were able to convert their own HTML to fully functional apps
  • Even though written before they knew about Mavo
  • Suggesting Mavo can work in real web designs
  • 3 liked the study so much they went home and built real apps
